Human Traffic Jam

News & Humor

Introduction

A humor video making light of SFU's never-ending construction.

I was tasked with creating a video showcasing and satirizing the intrusive construction ongoing at SFU.

Role

My role in this project was to edit the video.

My fellow Multimedia Editor was in charge of filming and passing on the footage to me. I also contributed some B-roll to the project as required.

Project Outcomes

This project was one of my first at The Peak.

It was very fun and since it concerned topical events at my own university I was intrigued and had a lot of laughs making it with my fellow colleagues at The Peak.
